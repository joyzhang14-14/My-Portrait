# Fixed Third Crisis and probed its Chinese setup

The user worked on getting Third Crisis Neon Nights running correctly in a CrossOver bottle and verifying whether it had usable Chinese localization. They confirmed a crash fix by disabling the Lovense Bluetooth plugin, checked that the process stayed stable for 41 seconds without another "Program Error," and inspected the title screen plus video, game, sound, controls, and toys settings pages. In Terminal, they searched ThirdCrisis_Data, found Chinese text counts in resources.assets and sharedassets0.assets, concluded the game contained embedded Chinese strings, and then experimented with Settings.sav language values such as 1, 40 (ChineseSimplified), and 6 while relaunching the game. They also verified that a ThirdCrisis.lnk shortcut had been created, but the game kept reopening in English, so they continued investigating how the language system was actually keyed.
